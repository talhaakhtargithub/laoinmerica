1. Eligibility for Refunds

1.1. Conditions:

  • Products must be returned in their original, unopened packaging.
  • Returns must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery date.
  • Proof of purchase (e.g., receipt or order confirmation) is required.

1.2. Exclusions:

  • Opened or used products are not eligible for a refund.
  • Clearance or sale items are non-refundable.

2. How to Request a Refund

2.1. Contact Us: To initiate a return, please contact us at info@laoinamerica.com with the following details:

  • Order number
  • Reason for return
  • Photo of the product (if applicable)

2.2. Return Shipping:

  • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective or we made an error in the order.
  • Please use a trackable shipping method to ensure safe delivery.

3. Refund Process

3.1. Inspection and Approval:

  • Once we receive your return, we will inspect the product and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

3.2. Processing Time:

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days.
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

4. Exchanges

4.1. Defective or Damaged Products:

  • If you receive a defective or damaged product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will replace the item or issue a refund at no additional cost.

4.2. Product Replacement:

  • Exchanges are only available for the same product, subject to stock availability.

5. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received your refund within the expected timeframe:

  • Check with your bank or credit card provider, as processing times may vary.
  • Contact us at info@laoinamerica.online for assistance.

6. Final Sale Items

Items marked as “final sale” are not eligible for returns, refunds, or exchanges.
